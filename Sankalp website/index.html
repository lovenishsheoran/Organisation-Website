About Us

Sankalp Enviro is a student organization under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare, Lovely Professional University. The name Sankalp Enviro drives its name from words, in which Sankalp means "to determine" and Enviro refers "environment”. As its name suggests, Sankalp Enviro's objective is to spread awareness on social and environmental related issues. The team of Sankalp has been thriving to provide practical solutions to these issues. It intends to bring along the masses together to protect the environment and carry out work at the ground level. We as a team of Sankalp like to be the change that we want to see. We started our journey on September 21st 2014 with few students of Lovely Professional University. We have been striving hard to bring change in society around us regarding the social responsibility and environment. Now we have grown to a good strength of 80+ from diverged culture but united for a common cause. As our helping hands grown the deeds we did also have grown big. The overview of our work is listed below.

Why to Choose Us

We think you should join us because you will explore your self. You might get to be a person who changed someone's life and bring hope. And moreover you will never regret that you never did anything for our mother nature.

DEDICATED TEAM

Our team consists of self motivated and enthusiastic students from different domains of education in our university who are always ready to serve and bring change in the society.

SHAPE YOUR IDEA

You get to have a platform to work out on your idea to do something for the society. We together will make your idea a reality.

BUILD YOUR NETWORK

We built our team with people from different parts our country. We collaborate with different organizations for a good cause. The more hands we join with, the more impacted our motto is.

BEST OUT OF WASTE

An initiative to exhibit successful examples of utilizing the waste materials into practical and handmade craft materials (models). This event mainly focused on developing environment friendly products in a given span of time with utmost innovation.

ENVIG-MANIA

We successfully conducted “ENVIG~MANIA 2014” which was an environmental-cum-general quiz, with a massive registrations of 200. Here we focused to check how much today’s youth is concerned about the environment and the social issues that are prevailing in the society.

Plastiki

During Coke Studio, we conducted a plastic waste pet bottles collection drive. The main motto of this was to make public aware that how much plastic they have been inducting into the nature. Through that we separated the plastic waste from the bio waste which is useful for recycling.

SANGAM

An annual mega event, held which saw a large crowd participating in various events and contributing in spreading awareness about environmental and social issues. This is mainly a combination of events in which we invite the participants to participate in various literary, fine arts etc competitions.
